# Global Mobilization (GM) Compatibility

## Overview

This compatibility module adds support for Global Mobilization - Cold War Germany civilian units to the Civilian Presence system.

## Purpose

Provides authentic 1980s German civilian populations from both East and West Germany for Cold War-themed missions.

## Civilian Presets

### East Germany Civilians (CIV_GM_GC)

- **Units**: 10 active units
- **Period**: 1980s
- **Includes**: Various civilian clothing variants and police officers
- **Region**: East Germany (German Democratic Republic)

### West Germany Civilians (CIV_GM_GE)

- **Units**: 11 active units (includes some East German units for variety)
- **Period**: 1980s
- **Includes**: Postal workers (DBP), police officers, police patrols, and general civilians
- **Region**: West Germany (Federal Republic of Germany)

## Total Units

**17 agent classes** across 2 German civilian presets

## Disabled Units

### East Germany (CIV_GM_GC)

- `gm_gc_civ_pilot_80_blk` - Civilian pilot

### West Germany (CIV_GM_GE)

- `gm_ge_ff_man_80_orn` - Fire fighter (orange uniform)
- `gm_ge_pol_pilot_p1_80_grn` - Police pilot
- `gm_ge_adak_pilot_80_sar` - ADAK pilot (search and rescue)

**Reason**: Pilots and specialized emergency services personnel are disabled to focus on general civilian population and basic police presence.

## Notes

- The West Germany preset intentionally includes some East German civilian variants to provide additional variety in civilian appearances
- All Units require the Global Mobilization CDLC
